Robo WAR

Description:

This is the time to join combat .Get your fighter BOTS ready and rage your own backyard war. Let your BOT fight it out! The motive of the competition is to construct a destructive bot which would challenge other competitors in a dual combat. Give your best to survive in there.

Team Size:

• Each team consist of maximum 5 members.
• The event is open for all colleges and members from different colleges may also form their team.

Rules:

• Every team must have fascinating & unique team name and a team leader only who can directly talk to the organising members.
• The duration of one match will be of maximum 10 minutes.
• Points will be rewarded on the basis of aggressiveness, strategy, throwing other bot out of arena and any other damages. Flame is not allowed moreover it any team can't destroy the wire of source power of their opponent .
• In case of any technical fault, a maximum of 2 timeouts (each of 3 minutes) will be given to rectify the fault.
• In case of failing in rectifying the bot, the opponent team will be declared as winner.
• Bots will be separated on the decision of organising members in case bots are stuck.
• The bot should be touching the ground throughout the war.

Bot Specifications:

• The dimension of bots must not exceed 60cmx50cmx50cm and maximum weight of 25 Kg.
• The bot may be wired or wireless.
• In case of wireless control, it should operate on dual frequency mode to avoid interference with opponent bot.

Disclaimer:

• In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the coordinators will be final.
• Necessary, immediate, unbiased, slight changes in rules may be done by organizing on the spot in order to run the event smoothly.
• Prize may be reduced if number of participating team is less than 5 and event may be cancelled if participating team is less than 2.
