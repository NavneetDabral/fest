Tower Making

OBJECTIVE:

To build the tallest stable tower using the given bamboo sticks and given clay.

EVENT PARAMETERS:

• Each team will be provided with the specified number of bamboo sticks and clay.
• The team building the tallest tower that is stable and can withstand its own self weight will be declared as winner. No additional load will be applied on the tower.

MATERIALS PROVIDED:

• 25 bamboo sticks, clay, cutter and a pencil
• No material other than the material provided should be used by the teams.

GENERAL RULES:

• The participants may overlap, cut or bent the bamboo sticks in whatever way they want.
• Maximum 4-5 participants are allowed in a team.
• Event time is 120 minutes.
• The rules may be changed during the event as they deem fit.

Disclaimer :

• In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the coordinators will be final.
• Necessary, immediate, unbiased, slight changes in rules may be done by organizing on the spot in order to run the event smoothly.
• Prize may be reduced if number of participating team is less than 5 and event may be cancelled if participating team is less than 2.
