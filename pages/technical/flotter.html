Flotter


OBJECTIVE:

To make a boat using given material in the specific time and balance the maximum weight.

EVENT PARAMETERS:

• Each team will be provided with the specified materials.
• The team will have to build a boat having balance the maximum weight.

MATERIALS PROVIDED:

• Bamboo sticks
• Fevicol
• String
• Paper
• Cutter
• Scale
• No material ,other than the material provided should be used by the teams.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

• The number of team members allowed are only 2 participants.
• Event time is 90 minutes.
• Team members may be from same college or different colleges.
• All the participants should carry a valid ID card of their respective institutions.
• Make the boat under given size.
• Once the boat enters the water the organizers are not responsible for any kind of damage to the boat.
• To balance the maximum weight on the boat is done by the participants itself.
