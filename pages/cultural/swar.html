FOLDS OF MELODY

Solo Singing


“words make you think a thought...music makes you feel a feeling....a song makes you feel a thought". solo singing competition is all hot and ready to be served on your plate guys, so i request all the singers to get ready. its time to show the world the magic in your voice. so come join us at cliffesto 2018 –“colours-folds of origami". and fill the scenery with the colours of your melody.

rules:


1. time alloted for each performance is 3-4 min.
2. participants will not be allowed to refer to lyrics while singing.
3. the participants will be judged on the basis of their voice quality,clarity and their way of presentation.
4. ther will be first round in which all types of songs are allowed.
5. every round will be an elimination round.
6. in second round only bollywood.
7. in third round a word or a sentence will be given and contestants have to sing song in relation with it. they will be given 5 minutes for preparation.
8. then final rounds in which five members will present themselves.
9. prize will be for 1st,2nd and third place.
