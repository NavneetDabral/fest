INVICTUS


Only the resilient will stay

Description

Gear yourself up with the best of your dancing skills and match up to the beat of music to become the ‘invictus’-the unconquered.

Rules

  • Maximum team size is “15”. Apart from this, there can be atmost 3 people for backstage help.
  • The teams must bring CD’s for the music (mp3 format) or songs in a pendrive and should submit to the respective coordinators 30 minutes before the performance.
  • Event will consist of two rounds and a final showdown.
  • The first round will be a freestyle round. Time limit will be 5+2 minutes.
  • The teams should make sure that they do not give the same performance in the auditions as well as in the further rounds.
  • 5 teams will be qualified for the second round.
  • Second round will be a prop based round. Teams will have to choose props of their choice from the given list of choices, and they will have to make the maximum and most innovative use of props. Time limit will be 4 minutes.
  • 2 teams will compete for the final showdown.
  • The final round will be a theme based round. The theme will be ‘colors’. They will have to present their dance on the above theme in given time duration. Time limit will be 5+2 minutes.
  • There is restriction on use of any fire or inflammable object, glass shattered on the stage. Negative points will be awarded in such cases.
