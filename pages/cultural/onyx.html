ONYX STPES


“SPIN TILL THE FLOOR SMOKES”
Dancing is an art which Enhances inner beauty of the person who wants to express their feelings. So we invite you to show your Skills to express yourself in this solo dance event in CLIFFESTO 2018 .

RULES:

  • Preliminary / Elimination round is a qualifying round for the event. Those participants who fail to appear for the preliminary round shall not be considered for the finals.
  • The contestant can perform on any song of their choice.
  • The maximum time allowed to each contestant is 5(+1) minutes (including the time for introduction).
  • The contestants have to arrange the play ready music cassettes/CDs of their own. The play ready music cassettes/CDs and playing electronic instrument should be installed 30 minutes before the actual performance. No extra time shall be given for this purpose.
  • The contestants have to arrange for necessary costumes on their own.

ROUNDS:

Deciding Round
  • The rules for the final round shall remain the same
  • The maximum time limit allowed to each contestant is 8(+2) minutes.(including the time for introduction)

NOTE:

  • In case the participant exceeds the time limit the Judges will be free to deduct the marks.
  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
