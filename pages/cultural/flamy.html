RULES:

1. Music must be edited to accommodate the routine/time limits. Requests for fast forwarding, pitch control, speed, fade in/outs will be denied.
2. Music for each entry must be recorded universally so it is compatible with all playback sound equipment.
3 Choreographic content must be suitable for family viewing. Any choreography displaying violence, social prejudice or overt sexual innuendo will result in a deduction of points as reflected on your master score sheets.
4 Rehearsal, prior to or during the competition, on the competition floor is strictly prohibited.
5 Entrances and exits to the performance can only be done from the designated wing area. Entering or exiting via the audience is strictly prohibited.
6- Dancers cannot compete for 2 or more separate studios/organizations or a combination of independent and studio/organization.
7-Three winners (1st, 2nd and 3rd) will be announced.
8-Performance done in auditions must not be repeated.
9-2 rounds will be conducted.

TIME LIMIT :

3+-1 minute maximum Time limit Timing begins when the first dancer enters the stage and ends when the last dancer exits. Entrances and exits without music and bows must be included in the time limit.
Five points will be deducted for every 15 seconds a routine is over the time limit. Deductions begin at 4 minutes
